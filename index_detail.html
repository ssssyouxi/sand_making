ARTIFICIAL SAND COMES FROM BEHIND TO WIN THE MARKET

With rapid development of urbanization and modernization, the usage of sand increases at the same speed.Even if there is a great demand, natural sand has been exhausted because of excessive exploitation, and unauthorized digging results in the destroyed rivers. In this condition, artificial sand plays a more important role in the industry, which satisfied high demand of infrastructure construction.

| Advantage

Quality product

Artificial sand with high surface energy and good hydrophilicity provides both continuous gradations and different mineral compositions for you to satisfy specific requirements.

Steady supply

Construction waste can be used for making artificial sand, which conforms the principle of recycling economy.

Mechanized production

Making artificial sand with advanced methods can ensure the products’ quality and keep the production controllable and stable.

Licensed operation

There is a legal representative and a premise in the artificial sand making industry, which makes the production legalization.

RICH CASES PROVE THE HONER OF SAND MAKING MACHINE

250tph granite crushing line in Zambia

Raw Material:   granite

Output Size:   0-5mm, 15-19mm, 19-37.5mm, 37.5mm-50mm

Application:   infrastructure construction

Equipment:   Vibrating Feeder GZD1100×4900, European Type Jaw Crusher PEW860, Hydraulic Cone Crusher HPT220, Sand Making Machine VSI 5X9532, Vibrating Screen 2YZS2160, 3YZS2160

Brief Introduction: :   Aiming at the 250tph granite crushing line, the company prepared European Type Jaw Crusher, Hydraulic Cone Crusher, Sand Making Machine, Vibrating Feeder and Vibrating Screen to satisfy customers‘ requirements. 200TPH granite crushing plant in South Africa

Output Size:   0-8mm, 0-3mm,3-6mm, 6-9mm, 9-13mm, 13-19mm, 19-26mm

Application:   road construction and concrete batching

VU Aggregate Making System for limestone in Shanxi, China

Raw Material:   limestone

Output Size:   3mm

Application:   high-grade concrete

Equipment:   VU-70 Aggregate Making System

Brief Introduction: :   For this VU-70 Aggregate Making System, limestone was sent to VU Vertical Shaft Impact Crusher by elevator and then went to Mode Control Screen after high-frequency crushing. The materials on the screen were sent to VSI crusher for secondary crushing and others meeting requirements were sent to VU Particle Optimizer for shaping. Dust Filter controlled the content of powder and the final products would be discharged and mixed by Humidification Mixer.
